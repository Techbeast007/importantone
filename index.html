Introduction

Coaching is a powerful process that unlocks potential and drives performance. It's a partnership between a coach and an individual or team, focused on achieving specific goals and maximizing personal or organizational effectiveness.

ICF Unlocked is a platform for coaching professionals to connect, learn, and grow. It provides access to resources, events, and a community of like-minded individuals passionate about coaching.

Coaching spans various branches, including executive coaching, leadership coaching, life coaching, career coaching, and more, each catering to specific needs and objectives.

To become a certified coach, you need to demonstrate mastery of core coaching competencies and meet specific eligibility criteria set by the International Coach Federation (ICF).

Erickson Coaching International and TASC are renowned organizations offering coaching training and certification programs, providing a pathway to a successful coaching career.
